# Grief Prevention Commands

Back to Plugin Details

AbandonClaim

Deletes the claim you're standing in.

Usage:/AbandonClaim

ClaimExplosions

Toggle if explosions are allowed in the claim.

Usage:/ClaimExplosions

Trust

Gives another player permission to edit in your claim.

Usage:/Trust <user>

UnTrust

Revokes any permissions granted to a player in your claim.

Usage:/UnTrust <user>

AccessTrust

Gives a player permission to use your buttons, levers, and beds.

Usage:/AccessTrust <user>

ContainerTrust

Gives a player permission to use your buttons, levers, beds, crafting gear, containers, and animals.

Usage:/ContainerTrust <user>

TrustList

Lists the permissions for the claim you're standing in.

Usage:/TrustList

SubdivideClaims

Switches your shovel to subdivision mode, so you can subdivide your claims.

Usage:/SubdivideClaims

RestrictSubclaim

Restricts a subclaim, so that it inherits no permissions from the parent claim.

Usage:/RestrictSubclaim

BasicClaims

Puts your shovel back in basic claims mode.

Usage:/BasicClaims

PermissionTrust

Grants a player permission to share his permission leve with others.

Usage:/PermissionTrust <user>

Untrust All

Removes all permissions for all players in your claim.

Usage:/Untrust All

AbandonAllClaims

Deletes all your claims.

Usage:/AbandonAllClaims

ClaimsList

Lists a player's claims and claim block details.

Usage:/ClaimsList

IgnorePlayer

Ignores a target player's chat messages.

Usage:/IgnorePlayer <user>

UnIgnorePlayer

Un-Ignores a target player's chat messages

Usage:/UnIgnorePlayer <user>

IgnoredPlayerList

Lists all players current ignored.

Usage:/IgnoredPlayerList

UnlockDrops

Allows other players to pick up items you dropped when you died.

Usage:/UnlockDrops
